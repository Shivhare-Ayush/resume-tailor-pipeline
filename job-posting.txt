Sophomore Leadership Internship
Explore. Observe. Be Inspired.

Are you a rising college sophomore or junior still figuring out your future career path? The Sophomore Leadership Internship Program is your chance to explore the professional world through job shadowing—a unique opportunity to observe industry professionals in action and discover what inspires you.

What This Program Is:

An 8-week immersive, paid job-shadowing experience in the automotive finance industry, designed to help you:

• Discover a variety of career paths

• Learn through observation and mentorship

• Build confidence in a professional setting

• Connect with peers and industry leaders

 

Responsibilities
﻿Learn about General Motors Financial and our various lines of business

What You Will Experience:

• Job Shadowing: Spend time alongside professionals in departments like Finance, Sales, Marketing, Pricing, Dealer Operations, and more.

• Mentorship: Receive guidance from experienced team members who are passionate about helping you grow.

• Leadership Development: Participate in workshops that build your confidence and leadership potential.

• Networking: Meet executives, peers, and professionals across the company.

• Diversity Equity & Inclusion: Learn about our commitment to DEI and how it shapes our workplace culture.

Location & Commitment:

• On-site in Fort Worth, Arlington, and Irving, TX

• Must reside in the Dallas/Fort Worth area

• Reliable transportation required

• Full participation for all 8 weeks is mandatory (May 27- July 17, 2026)

Qualifications
Who Should Apply:

• Rising sophomores or juniors (graduating Spring 2028–Spring 2029)

• Students enrolled full-time at an accredited college or university (HBCU or HSI preferred)

• Curious, engaged, motivated individuals who want to explore career options

• Minimum 3.0 GPA preferred

• Effective communication skills and ability to engage and commune with fellow cohort members

• Leadership experience is a plus—but not required!

GM Financial does not provide sponsorship for a work visa status for this position.
